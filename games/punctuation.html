What punctuation belongs in the highlighted spot?
Quick Rules:

Commas: After introductory phrases, in lists, before conjunctions (FANBOYS) in compound sentences.

Apostrophes: Contractions (don't = do not), possession (dog's toy), NOT for plurals.

Quotation Marks: Around direct speech, titles of short works. Periods/commas go inside.

End Marks: Period (.), question mark (?), exclamation point (!).
